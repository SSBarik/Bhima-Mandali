about Us ?


Bhima Mandali is a naturally enriched biodiverse destination situated in the Central Odisha.
It's also recognized as Bhima Gudi by the locals.
The place is credited for having the foot prints of Ancient Civilization to this Modern Age.


That's how it's also regarded for the initiation of Oldest Civilization of Central Odisha. The deep forest , flow of water , patches of sand at river beach & waterfall add charms to the glory of this spot.



The majestic beauty of Bhima Mandali have even captured the attention of some Foreign Tourists to here.Under the active guidence of Mr.Ramesh Prasad Swain this place is under the plannings for getting turned into an Ecotourism Spot

ODISHA ECOTOURISM DEVELOPMENT BOARD (OEDB)
Odisha Ecotourism Development Board is proposed to be the apex body in the state engaged in promotion of ecotourism and development of requisite system and standards for the same.

CONSTITUTION
  The Board shall have the following composition:
    1.Cheif Secretary - Chair person
    2.Secretary(Forest and Environment)
    3.Secretary(Tourism)
    4.Secratary(Finance)
    5.Secratary(Revenue)
    6.PCCF Odisha
    7.CLWL Odisha
    8.Nominated members
      -Travel and Tourism (1)
      -Wildlife Conservationist(1)
      -NGO(1)
    9.CEO/Member Secratary

FUNCTIONS

i. Plan, develop and facilitate ecotourism projects comaptible with this policy.
ii.Identifying and establishing an Odisha brand with unique value of the State, through consensus building process involving all stake holders.
iii.Developing and enforcing standards and norms for promotion and implementation of ecotourism destinations based in specific carrying capacity studies conducted.
iv.Identify and prioritize ecotourism destinations throughout the State based on parameters to be defined and approved by the Board.
v.Establish partnerships between various government departments, local communities and other stakeholders.
vi.Promote community based ecotourism enterprises in protected areas and other potential destinations through constituiton of "Ecotourism Societies" at the forest division/PA level.
vii.Capapcity building of various stakeholders.
viii.Develop marketing strategies and promote networking among ecotourism enterprises and operators.
ix.Facilitate education, research, monitoring and evaluation of the ecotourism activites.
